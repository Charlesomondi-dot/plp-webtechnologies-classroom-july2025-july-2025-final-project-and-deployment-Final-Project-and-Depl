My Journey

Hello! I'm Charles Omondi, an adaptable and enthusiastic Software Developer, Cyber Security Enthusiast, and UI/UX Designer pursuing a BSc in Computer Science at the University of Eldoret. My passion lies in utilizing technology to drive organizational growth and solve real-world challenges through innovative solutions.

As the Google Developers Group Lead for UI/UX at my university, I've led initiatives that have attracted over 50 attendees per event, organizing workshops on wireframing, prototyping, and user research. My journey spans from system administration to penetration testing, always ensuring safety in the software development lifecycle and enhancing robust system security.

I'm a Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) and hold Cisco Networking Basics certification, combining my development skills with cybersecurity expertise. When I'm not coding or testing systems, you'll find me swimming, hiking, solving puzzles to enhance my logical thinking, or gaming to improve my gamification skills in development.
